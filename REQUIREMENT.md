# Requirements

## Functional
- Daily planning with timeline blocks
- Money tracker: income/expense, categories, cycle-based budgeting
- Habit/daily tracker
- Searchable knowledge notes
- Simple reporting (weekly/monthly summary)

## Non-Functional
- Fast to capture (low friction)
- Data ownership and portability
- Works even when internet is unstable (future goal)
- Modular: can replace components without breaking everything
